Custom Printing Details

Once our representative connects with you, we can assist with the production of die lines and/or artwork if needed. Please follow the guide for file requirements during the quote process and final production.

Final print artwork file format and specifications:

  • Adobe Illustrator EPS vector file preferred
  • Please save all Adobe Creative Cloud Illustrator files as the Legacy Format CS6
  • Please ensure text is converted to outlines
  • Feel free to ask us for assistance if you have questions. We can assist and convert files from some software.
  • Images within the file must be embedded. If images are linked, please provide them. If links are NOT provided or embedded they will not show up on the proof.
  • Links and images must be at least 300 DPI (resolution). Lower resolution than 300 DPI at the actual size of print will cause lower quality printing of your image than shown on a computer screen.
  • It is helpful if artwork is on a die line of the bag or box to ensure proper logo placement. We can assist with the design and production of your product die line and artwork.
